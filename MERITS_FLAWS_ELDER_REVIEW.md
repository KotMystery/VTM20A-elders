# Merits & Flaws - Elder Appropriateness Review

This document lists all merits and flaws, with markers for those that **DO NOT FIT** the premise of Elders waking from centuries of torpor.

Legend:
- ⚠️ **PROBLEMATIC** - Doesn't fit Elder/torpor premise
- ✅ **APPROPRIATE** - Works fine for Elders
- ⚡ **CONTEXT-DEPENDENT** - Could work with proper justification

## RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Remove entirely** - Merits/flaws that are completely incompatible (generation flaws, computer aptitude, all Masquerade-related)
2. **Add warnings** - Merits/flaws that need context (True Love, Boons, Enemies - note that centuries have passed)
3. **Modify descriptions** - Some could work with adjusted text explaining they're from ancient times
